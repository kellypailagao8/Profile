About.

Hi! I’m Kelly, a student currently exploring the exciting world of web design and development. What inspires me the most is the ability to create websites that are visually appealing and easy to use and accessible to everyone.

My journey started when curiosity about how websites are built, which grew into a passionate learning experience. Experimenting with new tools, coding methods, and honing both design and problem-solving skills has become one of the most rewarding aspects of being a potential designer.

Outside of design, I love exploring photography, reading about tech trends, and sharing my thoughts with classmates, which often inspires my creativity and storytelling.

My ultimate goal is to combine creativity and technology to design websites that connect people, solve problems, and make the web a more beautiful place.
